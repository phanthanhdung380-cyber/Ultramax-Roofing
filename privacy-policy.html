Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you interact with this website. We are committed to transparency in our data practices and compliance with applicable United States federal and state privacy laws, including but not limited to the Federal Trade Commission Act Section 5, Privacy Act of 1974, Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), Colorado Privacy Act (CPA), Connecticut Data Privacy Act (CTDPA), and Utah Consumer Privacy Act (UCPA).

Categories of Personal Information Collected

We collect the following categories of personal information when you use our contact form or otherwise interact with our website:

  • Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and IP address.
  • Contact Information: Physical address if provided, email address, and telephone number.
  • Communications Data: The content of messages you send through our contact form or email.
  • Internet Activity Information: Browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring website addresses.

Sources of Information

Personal information is collected directly from you when you voluntarily provide it through our contact form, email correspondence, or phone calls. Technical information is collected automatically through your browser and device when you access our website.

Purposes of Data Collection and Use

We collect and use personal information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to service inquiries and questions submitted through the contact form.
  • Providing information about roofing services, materials, and project procedures.
  • Maintaining records of communications for quality assurance and reference purposes.
  • Operating and maintaining website functionality.
  • Analyzing website usage to understand visitor patterns and make content adjustments.
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting against fraud or security threats.

We do not use personal information for targeted advertising, cross-context behavioral advertising, or profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

Data Retention

Personal information submitted through the contact form is retained for the duration necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain records of service requests. Contact information may be retained for up to three years from the date of last communication to facilitate follow-up inquiries and maintain service history. Technical data collected through website analytics is retained for twelve months. After retention periods expire, personal information is securely deleted or anonymized.

Disclosure and Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information to third parties. We do not share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Personal information may be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with contractors or service providers who assist in business operations, such as website hosting or email services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect information and use it only for specified purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: Information may be disclosed when required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or when necessary to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, personal information may be transferred to the successor entity.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include secure server hosting, encrypted data transmission via HTTPS protocol, access controls limiting personnel who can view personal information, and regular security assessments. However, no electronic transmission or storage system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has submitted information to us should contact us using the contact details provided above.

Consumer Rights Under State Privacy Laws

Residents of certain U.S. states have specific rights regarding their personal information:

Rights for California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected, the sources from which it was collected, the purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom it is shared.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information.
  • Right to Limit: You may limit the use of sensitive personal information. We do not use sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide services.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You will not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your privacy rights.

Notice at Collection (California)

At the time of collection, we collect identifiers, contact information, communications data, and internet activity information as described above. This information is used for the purposes listed in this policy. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Rights for Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Utah

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Utah have similar rights to access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of their personal information, as well as the right to opt out of targeted advertising and the sale of personal information. As we do not engage in these activities, these opt-out rights are not applicable to our data practices.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of the rights described above, please contact us using the contact information provided at the beginning of this policy. We will verify your identity before processing requests. You may designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf by providing written authorization. We will respond to verified requests within the timeframes required by applicable law, typically 45 days, with the possibility of one extension if needed.

Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control

Some browsers include Do Not Track (DNT) features. Currently, there is no industry consensus on how to respond to DNT signals. We recognize and respect Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as an opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information where applicable under state law.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or website functionality. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact for Privacy Questions

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, our data practices, or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at the address, email, or phone number provided at the beginning of this policy.

For more information, please use the contact details above.
