What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They serve various functions, including remembering your preferences, enabling website features, and providing information about how visitors use a site. This Cookie Policy explains what types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses a limited number of cookies, categorized as follows:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic navigation, access to secure areas, and ensure pages load correctly. Without these cookies, certain website features would not be available. Strictly necessary cookies do not collect information about you for marketing purposes and cannot be disabled through our cookie settings.

Examples: Session management cookies that maintain your connection as you navigate between pages.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember choices you make to provide enhanced, personalized features. These may include remembering your language preference, regional settings, or text size selections. The information collected by functional cookies is typically anonymized and does not track your browsing activity on other websites.

Examples: Cookies that remember if you have dismissed notification messages or accepted cookie consent.

Limited Analytics Cookies

We use a minimal set of analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect aggregated, anonymous information about page views, time spent on pages, navigation paths, and referring sources. This data helps us identify which content is useful and where improvements might be made. Analytics data is not linked to individual identities and is used solely for internal website operation purposes.

Examples: Cookies that count page visits and measure website traffic patterns without identifying individual users.

Cookies We Do Not Use

This website does not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or cookies for cross-site behavioral advertising. We do not place cookies that track your browsing behavior across different websites. We do not share cookie data with advertising networks or third-party marketing services. No cookies on this site are used for targeted advertising, remarketing, or profiling activities.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include embedded content or links to external resources, such as maps, social media platforms, or document viewers. These third-party services may set their own cookies when you interact with embedded content. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy and cookie policies of any external sites you visit through links on our website.

Cookie Duration

Cookies used on this website fall into two categories based on how long they remain on your device:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. These are necessary for website functionality during your visit.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. Functional and analytics cookies may be persistent to remember your preferences or provide usage data over time. Persistent cookies on our site typically expire within one year.

Managing Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies on your device:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically set your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only certain types of cookies, or notify you when a cookie is being set. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect website functionality and prevent access to certain features.

Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Changing or Withdrawing Consent

If you previously accepted cookies on our website, you can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clearing existing cookies from your device. Changing cookie preferences will apply to your future visits to the website.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We recognize and respect Global Privacy Control signals transmitted by your browser. If your browser sends a GPC signal indicating you wish to opt out of data sharing or sales, we will honor that preference in accordance with applicable state privacy laws. Note that because we do not engage in data sales or cross-site tracking, GPC signals primarily serve as confirmation of your privacy preferences.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain types of cookies may impact your experience on our website:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Website may not function properly; some pages or features may be inaccessible.
  • Functional cookies: Preferences will not be remembered; you may need to re-enter settings on each visit.
  • Analytics cookies: Your visit will not be counted in our usage statistics; this does not affect your ability to use the site.

Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in cookie usage, technology, or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date indicates when the most recent revision was made. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our cookie practices.

Questions About Cookies

If you have questions about how we use cookies, our data practices, or how to manage your cookie preferences, please contact us using the contact information provided at the beginning of this policy.
